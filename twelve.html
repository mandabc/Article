Just like the kitchen, the bathroom received a total overhaul. Bumping it out an extra three-and-a-half feet in one direction allowed Safura to add a custom double vanity, a must for the client. Out went the bulky tub, in went a glassed-in shower. Last but not least was wall-to-wall mirror, plus an extra sliver, to make the room seem twice as big as it actually is. "I like continuity. I like this idea of mirror starting and finishing in a given space. I don’t like strips of mirror, I don’t like framed mirror," says Safura.
